We offer competitive prices, transparent evaluation, and instant cash payments with no hidden charges.

Q2. How long does the whole process take?
The entire process typically takes 15-30 minutes from evaluation to payment.

Q4. What is the charge for testing and purity evaluation?
Our testing and evaluation services are completely free of charge.
