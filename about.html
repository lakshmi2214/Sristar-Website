Our Founding Story

A Commitment to Fair Finance

Sristar Gold Company was born from a deeply personal experience. Our founder, Srikanth V. Nalige, faced a significant challenge when his family struggled to redeem pledged gold due to financial constraints. This experience ignited a passion to create a better solution for families facing similar difficulties. Driven by the belief that everyone deserves fair and accessible financial options, Srikanth established Sristar Gold Company. His dedication to this mission has been recognized with various awards and accolades in the gold buying industry.
