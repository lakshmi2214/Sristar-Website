This scheme is designed to help individuals who have pledged their gold elsewhere and want a way to either sell it securely or retrieve it on flexible terms.

Let's walk you through how this works, simply and transparently.

How the Sell and Save Scheme Works

If you've pledged your gold at any institution or agency and you're looking to get it back without hassle, we can help. Through our Sell and Save Scheme, Sristar Gold Company pays the full amount needed to release your gold—this includes all dues related to the pledge.

Once the gold is released, you get to choose what happens next:

  • If you want to sell the gold, we'll purchase it at the live market price and pay you the balance amount, after deducting what we spent on the release.
  • If you don't want to sell the gold, we'll safely store it and give you the option to repay us in easy monthly installments. You can choose repayment periods of 6, 9, or 12 months, based on what suits you best.

No pressure. No confusion. Just clear choices.

Your Gold, Your Decision

Many people hold deep emotional value in their gold, and we respect that. This scheme doesn't push you to sell—it empowers you with flexible options so you can make the best decision based on your situation.
